Have a Tech Issue?

The MU TechBot is designed to make dealing with tech problems a little easier for students. You can start by sending the TechBot a simple "hello" or you can go straight into the nitty gritty by saying what device you're having a problem with and what that issue is. For instance, "My computer won't start". The TechBot can help by giving you basic debugging solutions and if those don't work it can set up an appointment for you with Tiger Tech. The option to talk to the TechBot on the phone is also available, just call +1 240-258-7987.

The TechBot also knows a few special tricks so play around. And if you're feeling down about your broken tech, you can ask the TechBot to raise your spirits with some tech themed jokes. He may even give you some inspirational advice.
